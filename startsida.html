Advanced humanoid robots engineered for warehouse operations, factory assembly and construction work.

Why Workbot

Our workbots are designed to work alongside humans, not to replace them. By handling repetetive tasks, such as improving safety conditions, and learning from every interaction, our workbots help humans focus on what they do best: create, solve and innovate.

Application Areas

Warehouse: Our liftbots can perform task such as loading items, stacking items, taking inventory, tagging items and driving forklifts.
