Q&A

Question: What´s the purpose of booking a demo?

Answer: During the demo, you and your potential collegues will get to view all our different robots and their customization options. We will show you first hand what kinds of tasks they are able to perform and how it will revoluionze your workflow.

Question: Do you provide leasing solutions?

Answer: Yes we provide several different leasing solutions. For further details, contact us through our contact form.

Question: Do you provide service solutions for your bots?

Answer: Yes we do. However, there are also multiple specialized companies providing similar services.

Question: Is it possible to add customizations after use?

Answer: Yes, however we require a full service inspection with potential repairs before adding new customizations.

Question: Do you provide student visits?

Answer: Yes but with a capacity limit. In order to book a student visit you will have join the wait list. To join the wait list, go to “book a demo” and check the option “student visit”, then fill out the form and send it to us.
